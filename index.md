Mandatory:

1. Andrew S. Tanenbaum, Modern Operating Systems, 4th Ed., Pearson Educational Limited, 2015. ISBN 10: 1-292-06142-1. Chapters: 1-7, 12.
2. All lecture notes.
3. All projects.
